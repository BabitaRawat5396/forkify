CARROT CAKE

90 MINUTES

1 servings

  • 75g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsps cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 50g soft brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 62 1/2 ml oil
  • 1/4 orange zested
  • 1/4 lemon zested
  • 50g carrots finely grated
  • 37 1/2g walnuts chopped
  • 56 3/4g tin pineapple pieces well drained and chopped
  • 31 1/4g unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 12 1/2g icing sugar
  • 62 1/2g cream cheese
